Research:

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Kempner Institute and School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS), Harvard University. I study imitation learning and interactive learning—or, more broadly, settings that involve a feedback loop between a machine learning agent and the input the machine learning agent sees.

Prospective Students:

I am actively recruiting new PhD students with a strong background in applied reinforcement learning, natural language processing, machine learning, and deep learning. If you are applying to Harvard, please mention my name in your research statement. For already admitted Harvard PhD students, please send me your CV and a brief description of your research interest.
